What we are looking for

High academic acheivers

we are looking at individual's exceptional performance in their academic pursuits. It signifies a level of excellence and mastery in academic endeavors, reflecting a strong commitment to learning, intellectual growth, and the acquisition of knowledge.

Possesion of strong leadership qualities

We work with college students who share set of skills, attributes, and behaviors that enable them to effectively guide, inspire, and influence others towards a common goal within a college or university setting and we expect that leadership qualities can be developed and strengthened over time through self-reflection, learning from experiences, seeking feedback,and actively engaging in leadership roles and opportunities.

Extraordinary Extracurricular activities

Extracurricular activities are voluntary, non-academic pursuits that students engage in outside of their regular academic curriculum. These activities typically take place within the school or college environment and offer students opportunities to explore their interests, develop new skills, and engage with their peers in a more informal and recreational setting.We are looking for students who engaged in sports, performing arts, student led organisation,academic competions, volunteer activities etc

Who we are

Brief description and background

Welcome to EmpowerEd, an online a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering and supporting low-income, high-achieving students in their pursuit of higher education. We firmly believe that financial barriers should never hinder talented individuals from reaching their full academic potential.As EmpowerEd, we understand the immense value of a master's degree in shaping individuals' futures and opening doors to endless opportunities. Our mission is to bridge the gap between deserving students and the scholarship opportunities that can transform their lives.We work tirelessly to connect these talented students with a comprehensive range of scholarships specifically tailored for master's programs. Through our extensive network of partners, foundations, we strive to create a level playing field where financial constraints are no longer a barrier to success.

Our dedicated team of professionals is committed to providing personalized guidance and support throughout the entire scholarship application process. We offer comprehensive resources, including onlinne workshops, mentorship programs, and application assistance, to ensure that each student maximizes their chances of securing the financial aid they deserve.What sets EmpowerEd apart is our focus on serving students from low-income backgrounds who have demonstrated exceptional academic achievements for free. We firmly believe that everyone deserves an equal opportunity to excel academically and contribute meaningfully to society.

Join us in our mission to empower these exceptional individuals by becoming a part of our community. Whether you are a passionate student seeking scholarship opportunities or an organization willing to make a difference, your involvement can create lasting impact and change lives.
